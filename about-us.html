What’s The Citi Brewing?

ABOUT

Citi Brew is a New York tech company, connecting locals with a new way to access an endless variety of craft beverages in their area. Our mission is to provide all adults (21 and older) with a variety of locally crafted beverages shipped to your home or business at the click of a button.
